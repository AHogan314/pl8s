About

Plates is a web app project built to demonstrate PHP, SQL, HTML and CSS, along with the Bootstrap framework.

Have you ever wanted to give a motorist feedback? Have you ever wondered how other motorists might critique your driving? With Plates, want and wonder no more! Submit anonymous critiques to our database, or look up a license plate to see what critiques others have submitted.
