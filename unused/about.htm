What is Socialife?

Socialife is a web application for managing your social interactions, not across a single platform, but across your entire life.
There are myriad ways in modern times to interact with your friends, family, coworkers, acquaintances, and so on. But how do you manage these communication channels? When is the last time you spoke to that friend from high school, that coworker from your last job, your mother? Have any of your social connections slipped through the cracks? You have 800 friends on Facebook, but how many of them actually consider you a friend?
Socialife's aim is to help you manage those interactions, be it an instant message, phone call, email, Facetime or face-to-face.
Create a simple contact profile using the submission form. The form asks for only basic information: first and last name, type of acquaintance, and a tag or two describing positive and negative aspects or topics of your most recent conversation. Your contacts are displayed as a list, ordered by most recent interaction. Existing contacts can be updated as your daily life plays out.
Currently your Socialife contacts can only be managed manually, but in the future this service could use social media platform APIs, or perhaps a mobile application with SMS and phone call permissions, to automatically update your contacts as interactions happen.
